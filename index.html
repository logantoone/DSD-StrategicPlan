About the Strategic Plan
The Davis School District Strategic Plan serves as a guide for the district's priorities and continuous improvement efforts. Organized around three core focus areas—Academic Achievement, Community Connection, and Operational Excellence—the plan identifies the philosophies, goals, and key indicators that support student success and organizational effectiveness. The Board of Education uses the strategic plan to align policies, resources, programs, and accountability measures while providing a shared direction for the work of the district.
Academic Achievement
Learning is the foundation of everything we do. Effective educators provide powerful learning opportunities that challenge, support, and inspire all students. As students receive effective instruction, they acquire a comprehensive body of academic knowledge and develop skills essential for success in their future education, careers, community, and life.
Supporting Goals
1
Promote high-quality instruction in every setting for every student
2
Support responsible use of digital tools
3
Provide high-quality intervention and enrichment to meet the learning needs of all students
4
Implement evidence-based curriculum effectively in every classroom
5
Support students in meeting grade-level academic standards and graduating with the knowledge and skills needed for success in college, careers, community, and life
6
Establish systems, structures, and supports that promote student engagement
Key Indicators
  • Graduation rates
  • College and career readiness
  • Literacy outcomes
  • Academic proficiency and growth
  • Attendance rates
  • Student behavior
  • Program evaluation results
  • Learning experience data
Community Connection
As the center of our communities, DSD schools provide a foundation of belonging and relationships that are essential to academic success. Our schools are welcoming; students, teachers, parents, and staff feel safe, supported, and connected. School staff foster strong, caring relationships with students, promote the safe sharing of ideas, and work collaboratively with parents and administrators to help each student succeed. We continue to treat all with respect and civility while building trust within our school communities.
Supporting Goals
1
Foster connections between every student and a caring staff member so each feels included, valued, and dignified in their daily school experience
2
Provide students access to leadership opportunities and support full participation in school learning and activities within safe environments
3
Ensure parents feel welcomed, empowered, and comfortable engaging with school leadership as valued members of the school community
4
Advance student and staff safety, belonging, and connection
5
Provide varied opportunities for students to connect through participation in clubs, athletic teams, and extracurricular activities
6
Strengthen collaborative relationships that support every student's academic growth and success
Key Indicators
  • Effectiveness Assessment Report data
  • Participation in clubs, athletics, and extracurricular activities
  • Community focus group feedback
  • Community Council and PTA feedback
  • Graduation rates, attendance rates, and academic performance data
  • Student and staff retention rates
Operational Excellence
Excellence is achieved through intentional systems that equip our employees with the training, tools, and support needed to serve students at the highest level. We prioritize clear communication and transparency to build trust and foster a shared responsibility for success. We demonstrate responsible use of public resources through sound decision-making and fiscal stewardship. Well-designed academic, behavioral, and operational systems reduce barriers and allow employees and students to focus on learning, growth, and service. We are committed to physical, emotional, and digital safety, creating sustainable excellence in environments where students and employees can thrive.
Supporting Goals
1
Maintain a comprehensive system of tools, training, and coaching that develops employee expertise and supports high performance across roles
2
Support employees in delivering effective academic and behavioral strategies that directly impact student achievement
3
Promote communication that is regular, transparent, appropriate, and grounded in integrity with stakeholders
4
Recognize excellence and proactively share the great work of employees
5
Advance systems of operational excellence through informed decision-making, fiscal responsibility, and wise stewardship of public resources
6
Maintain a safe and secure environment that protects the physical, emotional, and digital well-being of all students and employees
Key Indicators
  • Employee training and professional development participation
  • Professional development offerings in professionalism, fiscal responsibility, compliance, academics, culture, and behavior
  • Curriculum adoption and implementation processes
  • Allocation of personnel, materials, and facility resources
  • Stakeholder communication across multiple platforms
  • Policy alignment and regular updates
  • Stakeholder feedback systems and responsiveness
  • Recognition of employee excellence
  • Annual audit reports
  • Financial transparency and balanced budgeting
  • Alignment of fiscal resources to strategic goals
  • Culture and climate data
  • Student digital safety systems and data
  • Physical safety systems and emergency response protocols
  • Employee evaluation system data
